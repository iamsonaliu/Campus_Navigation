About Us

Welcome to CAMPUSNAV-AI, a project developed by a team of passionate students from the Hill Campus. Our goal is to make navigation seamless for students, faculty, and visitors, both inside and outside the campus. We’ve integrated AI-powered pathfinding, live location mapping, and a chatbot to assist Hill Campus students with their daily needs.

Our Team

We are a group of dedicated students specializing in computer science and engineering. Our team includes developers, designers, and researchers working together to bring CAMPUSNAV-AI to life. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to enhance this platform!
